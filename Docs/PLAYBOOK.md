# Recipe App Playbook

## Vision

Help people feel confident enough to cook.

## Product Principles

- Every feature earns its place.
- Get me cooking, not reading.
- Design for confidence, not complexity.
- Every screen should increase confidence.
- The app should feel like a companion that comes to life in your kitchen.

## Questions We're Exploring

- How should Make Now work?
- Should Favorites come before Boards?
